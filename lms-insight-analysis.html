InsightGrade

AI-Assisted Academic Evaluation Infrastructure — end-to-end systems designed to reduce educator workload and improve evaluation quality.

We are
AI-assisted academic evaluation infrastructure. Educators assist, humans decide.

Academic evaluation is one of education's most time-intensive, high-stakes workloads. Professors spend hours grading discussion threads — assessing reasoning depth, engagement quality, citation rigor, and originality — often with inconsistent rubric alignment and limited bandwidth for meaningful feedback.

InsightGrade is built to change that. It is infrastructure — not a grading shortcut. Each evaluation passes through a multi-model AI system that analyzes thinking quality, generates structured breakdowns with justification, and surfaces everything for professor review before anything is finalized or exported.

The result is more consistent, higher-quality feedback delivered at scale — while keeping the professor firmly in control at every step.

Operational Infrastructure

What We Are Building

The backend systems and operational layers that make evaluation infrastructure possible at institutional scale.

Secure & Compliant
FERPA-aligned data handling, encryption, and privacy by design. Academic data is treated with institutional-grade security standards at every layer of the system.
Data Processing Layer
Scalable ingestion and processing of discussion content and rubrics. Built to handle diverse LMS export formats and normalize them into structured evaluation-ready input.
AI Evaluation Engine
Multi-model AI system optimized for deep academic reasoning and rubric alignment. Evaluates critical thinking, engagement quality, citations, originality, and peer interaction — not just surface participation.
Professor Memory
Continuous learning of grading patterns, preferences, and rubric interpretation. The system adapts to each professor's standards over time — personalizing evaluation to their voice, not replacing it.
Analytics & Insights
Dashboards for educators and institutions to track evaluation quality and impact over time. Surfaces patterns in student engagement, feedback effectiveness, and rubric performance at class and department level.
Integrations
Seamless sync with LMS platforms — Canvas live, more coming. Grades and feedback export directly to the gradebook, removing manual re-entry and keeping institutional workflows intact.
What This Infrastructure Drives
Higher quality feedback Consistent, fair grading Reduced educator workload & burnout Stronger student learning outcomes Institutional confidence & adoption
