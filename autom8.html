Autom8: AI-Powered Instagram Client Acquisition Infrastructure.

Autom8 is Bare Branding Systems’s forward-facing AI automation platform built to help businesses turn Instagram comments into conversations, bookings, leads, and sales. It represents our evolution from traditional social media marketing into scalable AI-backed client acquisition systems.

Visit autom8ig.io ↗ Talk to the team
What it is

A client acquisition layer for the platform you’re already on.

Most service businesses, creators, and agencies already have the audience they need. The bottleneck is what happens between a comment and a conversation — and a conversation and a booking.

Autom8 is the AI layer that closes that gap. Comments become qualified DMs. DMs become warm conversations. Conversations become booked calls and paid clients — without the founder living inside their inbox.

It’s the productised version of an automation pattern we’ve been building inside Bare Branding Systems for years.

What Autom8 does

Built for client acquisition, not just engagement.

01

Comment-to-DM automation

Trigger personalised DMs from Instagram comments based on intent, keyword, post, or campaign — without sounding like a bot.

02

AI conversation flows

Qualifying questions, objection handling, and routing — designed specifically for service businesses, creators, and high-touch offers.

03

Booking & CRM handoff

Hot leads route to your calendar or CRM with full context, so your team picks up a warm conversation — not a cold ticket.

04

Reporting that matches reality

Funnel-level visibility from comment → DM → booking → revenue, so you actually know which content earns clients.

05

Brand-safe AI

Trained on your voice, your offer, and your guardrails. AI that sounds like you — not like everybody else’s AI.

06

Built by operators

Designed by a company that has actually run service businesses and content engines — not a generic chatbot vendor.

Service businesses, creators, and high-touch agencies.
